Detailed Description

Used to calculate fees in a polymorphic manner.

If you wish to pay fees in an asset other than CORE, use the core_exchange_rate argument to specify the rate of

exchange rate. It is up to the caller to ensure that the core_exchange_rate converts to an asset accepted by the delegates at a rate which they will accept.
