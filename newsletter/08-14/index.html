Newsletter 09 - August 2014!

 

If you missed our earlier newsletters you can find them at bitshares.org. You can get all the real-time breaking news on the forum at bitsharestalk.org. This newsletter focuses on strategic news - what’s on tap for the coming months.

 
 

Striking Distance!

BitSharesX has already moved into the Top Five at coinmarketcap.com.

And we are just getting started! Here are several reasons why we expect to completely reshuffle
the front page at coinmarketcap.com in the next few months.
 

Full Feature Set

 

Before the end of the summer, BitSharesX will upgrade to its full Version 1.0 feature set.
The ability to trade bitAssets (bitUSD, bitGLD, bitBTC, etc.) will be an industry game changer. This will grab plenty of headlines as traders rush to gain competitive edge by being among the first to exploit the stunning new potential.

 

Marketing Begins

BitSharesX reached the Top Six with historically low levels of marketing.  Now that we have a product, “history is gonna change”. Two independent marketing teams will be going after the Eastern and Western markets aggressively. Starting from near zero, we don’t think it will be hard to increase the demand for BTSX by 2x, 3x, or 10x with the tools, expertise, and strategies they now have at their disposal.

 

More Snapshots!

In parallel with the rise of BitSharesX, we will be headlining multiple new products bearing the BitShares brand throughout the summer and fall. BitShares DNS and BitShares VOTE will be taking their snapshots this month on August 21st. BitShares PTS sneaking toward the Top 10 in anticipation of free shares in many new offerings. BitShares Play and BitShares Music will also announce their snapshots soon. Six BitShares products debuting in the Top 20 will probably grab some new eyeballs.
